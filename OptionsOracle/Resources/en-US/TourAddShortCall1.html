Add "Short Call" Position (1/2)

To add options position to the strategy-table, drag the selected option from the options-table and drop it in the strategy-table.

How to drag-and-drop: move the mouse over the selected option, press the left-button, move the mouse to the strategy-table (while keeping left-button pressed !), release the mouse button.

To continue drag one of the call options from the options-table to the strategy.
